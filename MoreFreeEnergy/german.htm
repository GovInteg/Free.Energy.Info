[571 ABSTRACT

An alternating electric current generator comprises an armature rotatably carried by a

drive shaft and positioned between stabilized. non-moving magnetic elements. The end

armature has first and second magnetic field transmitting sections with a magnetized

section sandwiched there between. As electric load is applied to the generator, a countermagnetic

field is generated through the armature to increase speed of the drive

shaft and thereby lessen torque required to rotate the drive shaft.
